Life before programming

by Josue Hoenicka

Here's a time line of Josue's life:

  • 2003 - Born Josue in Caracas, Venezuela
  • 2005 - He moves to another city (Miranda, Venezuela)
  • 2007 - He passes quickly to primary school with 5 years old
  • 2012 - He moves to another city (Tachira, Venezuela)
  • 2013 - He finishes elementary school
  • 2013 - He returns to his hometown
  • 2014 - He starts high school
  • 2018 - He goes to Argentina
  • 2019 - He graduated from high school
  • 2020 - He works as a barber
  • 2021 - He becomes bilingual with Portuguese
  • 2022 - He begins his roadmap in programming

"Cuando escuché aquel famoso pensamiento que dice que: la mejor forma de predecir el futuro es crearlo, decidí cambiar mi rutina diaria y retarme a mí mismo, actualmente me encuentro saliendo de mi zona de confort constantemente"

Josue Hoenicka, 2022
